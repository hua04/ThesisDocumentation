9/3/24 - Domains and Precidents

In order to understand our reasearch focus and the context our work should relate to, we did this exercise in defining our areas of interest and finding prexisting work that would be related. This ensures that not only is the work we produce within our thesis original and innovative, but also that it contributes to a related field or context that already exists within the field. I personally found brainstorming the domains and concepts very easy, but struggled a bit more when it came time to look for precidents. I had to loosen my definition of certain concepts and expand my perspective in order to gather enough precidents, which I think will be helpful in the long run as it has encouraged me to be more flexible and try routes I wouldn't normally consider. It was also interesting to see how certain precidents repeated multiple times, or even got grouped together with others as they appeared in different domains together.
